Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products, shipping, returns, and more.

Orders & Shipping

How can I track my order?
▼
Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also log into your account and view your order status in the "My Orders" section. Tracking information is usually available within 24 hours of shipment.
How long does shipping take?
▼
Standard shipping takes 3-7 business days, while express shipping delivers in 1-3 business days. International orders typically arrive within 7-14 business days. Processing time is 1-2 business days before shipment.
Can I change or cancel my order?
▼
You can modify or cancel your order within 2 hours of placing it. After this window, orders enter processing and cannot be changed. Contact our customer service team immediately if you need assistance.
Do you offer free shipping?
▼
Yes! We offer free standard shipping on orders over $75 within the continental US. Express shipping is available for an additional fee. International shipping rates vary by destination.
↩️

Returns & Exchanges

What is your return policy?
▼
We offer a 30-day return policy for most items in original condition. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and include all original tags and packaging. Some exceptions apply to personalized items and final sale products.
How do I start a return?
▼
Log into your account, go to "My Orders," and select "Return Items" next to your order. Follow the prompts to print a prepaid return label. Package your items securely and drop off at any authorized shipping location.
When will I receive my refund?
▼
Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days after we receive your return. The refund will appear on your original payment method within 5-10 business days, depending on your bank or credit card company.
🛍️

Products & Sizing

How do I find the right size?
▼
Each product page includes a detailed size chart. We recommend measuring yourself and comparing to our charts rather than relying on standard sizing. If you're between sizes, we generally recommend sizing up for comfort.
Are your products authentic?
▼
Yes, we guarantee 100% authentic products. We work directly with authorized distributors and brands. All items come with authenticity certificates where applicable, and we stand behind our authenticity guarantee.
Do you restock sold-out items?
▼
Most popular items are restocked regularly. You can sign up for restock notifications on any sold-out product page. We'll email you as soon as the item becomes available again.

Payment & Security

What payment methods do you accept?
▼
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and Shop Pay. We also offer buy-now-pay-later options through Klarna and Afterpay.
Is my payment information secure?
▼
Absolutely. We use industry-standard SSL encryption and are PCI DSS compliant. Your payment information is never stored on our servers and is processed through secure payment gateways only.
Can I use multiple payment methods?
▼
Currently, we accept one payment method per order. However, you can use gift cards in combination with another payment method to cover any remaining balance.

Account & Support

How do I create an account?
▼
Click "Sign Up" at the top of any page and enter your email and password. You can also create an account during checkout. Having an account lets you track orders, save favorites, and checkout faster.
I forgot my password. What should I do?
▼
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and enter your email address. We'll send you a secure link to reset your password. If you don't see the email, check your spam folder.
How can I contact customer service?
▼
You can reach us via the contact form below, email at support@store.com, or phone at 1-800-123-4567 (Mon-Fri 9AM-6PM EST). We typically respond to emails within 24 hours.
